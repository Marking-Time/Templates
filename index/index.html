A Note on style:

One of the major reasons to hire a developer is to get the style you want rather than a style generated by AI or using a standard framework. This site and those in my portfolio reflect my personal style which is rather spare and industrial. For this reason it is highly recomended that you hire a professional graphic designer to design your site, or at the minimum create a branding kit for you (incliuding a logo, color pallete and fonts). You will get a better product and I get to spend more time on making it work which is what I like to do.
